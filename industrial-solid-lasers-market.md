<p><ul><li>Pulse Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial solid lasers are categorized into two primary types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers emit energy in short, intense bursts, making them suitable for applications requiring precision and high peak power, such as material cutting and engraving. Continuous type lasers, on the other hand, provide a steady output of energy, ideal for applications like welding and surface treatment where consistent power is necessary. Both types are crucial in various industries, enhancing efficiency and achieving desired outcomes in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
