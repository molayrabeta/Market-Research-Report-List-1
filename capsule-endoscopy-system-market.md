<p><p>The Capsule Endoscopy System market consists of three primary types: Capsule Endoscopes, Workstations, and Recorders. Capsule Endoscopes are small, ingestible cameras that capture images of the gastrointestinal tract. Workstations are the software and hardware platforms that facilitate the analysis and interpretation of the images collected by the capsules. Recorders are devices that store the video data transmitted from the capsule as it moves through the digestive system, ensuring a comprehensive examination and accurate diagnostics.</p></p>
