<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Transdermal Skin Patches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drug in Adhesive</li><li>Matrix</li><li>Reservoir</li><li>Vapor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transdermal skin patches are categorized based on their drug delivery mechanisms. </p><p>1. **Drug-in-Adhesive** patches incorporate the medication directly into the adhesive layer, allowing for direct contact with the skin.</p><p>2. **Matrix Patches** feature a drug dispersed within a polymer matrix, enabling controlled release as the drug diffuses through the skin.</p><p>3. **Reservoir Patches** contain a separate drug reservoir, allowing for a consistent release rate via a rate-controlling membrane.</p><p>4. **Vapor Patches** deliver medications through vapor diffusion.</p><p>5. **Others** may include innovative designs or hybrid systems combining various elements.</p></p>
