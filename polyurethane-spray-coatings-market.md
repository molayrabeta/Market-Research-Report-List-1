<p><p>Polyurethane spray coatings are categorized into several types, primarily solvent-based, water-based, and other formulations. Solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents, offering durability and resistance but with higher VOC emissions. Water-based polyurethane coatings are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower VOC levels and easier cleanup, making them suitable for indoor applications. Other types may include specialty formulations tailored for specific needs, such as hybrid systems or UV-resistant coatings, catering to diverse industry requirements while maintaining protective qualities and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
