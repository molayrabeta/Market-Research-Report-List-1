<p><strong>The Adjustable Fiber Collimator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Ferrule Contactor/Physical Contact (FC/PC)"</li><li>"Ferrule Contactor/Angled Physical Contact (FC/APC)"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adjustable Fiber Collimator market includes two primary types: Ferrule Connector/Physical Contact (FC/PC) and Ferrule Connector/Angled Physical Contact (FC/APC). FC/PC connectors are designed for standard fiber connections, ensuring minimal signal loss through direct contact. In contrast, FC/APC connectors feature an angled end face, which reduces reflections and enhances performance in high-speed applications. Both types are crucial for precise fiber optic alignment and are widely used in telecommunications, data centers, and other optical systems to ensure efficient signal transmission.</p></p>
