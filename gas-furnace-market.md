<p><ul><li>Single-stage Gas Furnaces</li><li>Two-stage Gas Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas furnace market is primarily categorized into single-stage and two-stage gas furnaces. Single-stage gas furnaces operate at a fixed output level, providing consistent heating but lacking efficiency in variable temperature conditions. In contrast, two-stage gas furnaces can adjust their heating output, offering improved efficiency and comfort by operating at lower levels during milder weather. This adaptability results in reduced energy consumption, making two-stage furnaces increasingly popular among consumers seeking energy efficiency and enhanced climate control.</p></p>
