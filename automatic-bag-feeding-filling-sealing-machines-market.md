<p><p>Automatic Bag Feeding Filling-Sealing Machines are categorized into three main types based on their motion mechanisms: </p><p>1. **Box-motion**: Utilizes a back-and-forth motion for precise bag handling, ideal for larger bags and complex filling tasks.</p><p>2. **Rotary-motion**: Employs a circular motion system, allowing continuous operation and high-speed filling, suitable for various bag sizes and types.</p><p>3. **Intermittent-motion**: Features temporary pauses during operation, providing accuracy and flexibility for diverse applications and batch processing needs. Each type addresses specific production requirements in the packaging industry.</p></p>
