<p><strong>The Patient Record Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patient Record Management is categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise systems are installed locally within healthcare facilities, offering complete control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Conversely, Cloud-Based solutions store patient records on remote servers, providing accessibility from various devices, lower initial costs, and automatic updates. While Cloud-Based systems facilitate collaboration and scalability, On-Premise options are often preferred for organizations prioritizing data sovereignty and control.</p></p>
