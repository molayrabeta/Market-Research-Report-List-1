<p><p>The microvolume spectrophotometer market encompasses various types based on the wavelengths utilized for analysis. Visible light spectrophotometers measure absorbance in the visible spectrum, primarily for colorimetric assays. UV spectrophotometers target short wavelengths for nucleic acid and protein quantification. Infrared (IR) spectrophotometers analyze molecular vibrations for material characterization. Fluorescence spectrophotometers detect emitted light from excited molecules, enabling sensitive detection of biomolecules. Each type serves distinct applications in research, healthcare, and industrial sectors, driving demand in the market.</p></p>
