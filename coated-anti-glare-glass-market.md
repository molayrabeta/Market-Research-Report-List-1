<p><p>The coated anti-glare glass market is divided into two main types: chemical treatment and physical processing. Chemical treatment involves applying specialized coatings that reduce glare by altering the glass surface at a molecular level, enhancing visual comfort and clarity. In contrast, physical processing includes methods such as sandblasting or etching to create a textured surface that diffuses light and minimizes reflections. Both techniques aim to improve visibility and reduce eye strain in various applications, such as automotive, architectural, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
