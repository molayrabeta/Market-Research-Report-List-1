<p><p>The DDoS Protection Services Market comprises various types of attack mitigation strategies tailored to different attack vectors. UDP Flood attacks overwhelm networks by flooding them with User Datagram Protocol packets, while ICMP Flood targets by bombarding systems with Internet Control Message Protocol packets. SYN Flood attacks exploit the TCP handshake process, sending numerous synchronization requests. HTTP Flood involves overwhelming web servers with excessive HTTP requests, disrupting service. Other types include application-layer attacks and amplification attacks, reflecting the diverse threats faced by organizations.</p></p>
