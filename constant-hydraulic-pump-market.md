<p><ul><li>Axial Pumps</li><li>Radial Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The constant hydraulic pump market is primarily divided into two types: axial pumps and radial pumps. Axial pumps utilize a design where fluid flows parallel to the pump shaft, providing high efficiency and flow rates, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control. Radial pumps, on the other hand, direct fluid flow perpendicular to the shaft, offering robust pressure generation and compact designs suitable for applications with limited space. Both types serve diverse industries, ensuring effective fluid power management.</p></p>
