<p><p>The Automatic Washer Disinfector market is divided into two main types: Cabinet (Single Chamber) Machines and Continuous Process Machines. Cabinet machines operate in a single, enclosed chamber, efficiently cleaning and disinfecting instruments in batches, making them ideal for smaller settings. In contrast, Continuous Process Machines feature a streamlined design that allows for the continuous flow of instruments through multiple stages of washing and disinfection, ensuring higher throughput, making them suitable for larger medical facilities with high demand.</p></p>
