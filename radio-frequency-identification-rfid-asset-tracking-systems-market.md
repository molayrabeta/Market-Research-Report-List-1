<p><ul><li>Active RFID Type</li><li>Passive RFID Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) asset tracking systems are categorized into two main market types: active and passive RFID. </p><p>Active RFID systems use battery-powered tags that continuously transmit signals, allowing for real-time tracking over longer distances. They are ideal for high-value assets or environments requiring constant monitoring. Conversely, passive RFID systems do not have their own power source; instead, they rely on energy from a RFID reader to transmit data. This type is generally more cost-effective and suited for applications with less frequent tracking needs.</p></p>
