<p><p>The semiconductor-grade wet chemicals market can be categorized into two main types: universal and functional. Universal wet chemicals are general-purpose solvents and cleaning agents used across various semiconductor manufacturing processes, ensuring overall cleanliness and performance. In contrast, functional wet chemicals are specialized formulations designed for specific applications, such as etching, doping, or surface conditioning. These differentiated products cater to the precise requirements of semiconductor fabrication, enhancing efficiency and yield in the production of microelectronics.</p></p>
