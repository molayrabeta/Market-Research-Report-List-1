<p><p>The 3 ply disposable medical mask market is primarily divided into woven cloth and non-woven segments. Woven cloth masks feature tightly knit fabric layers that offer moderate filtration, often used in settings requiring less strict protection. In contrast, non-woven masks, made from layered synthetic materials, provide superior filtration efficiency and are preferred in medical environments. Their melt-blown middle layer enhances barrier protection against pathogens, making non-woven masks the industry standard for healthcare professionals and high-risk scenarios.</p></p>
