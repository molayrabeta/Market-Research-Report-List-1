<p><p>Medication adherence packaging systems are designed to enhance patient compliance in taking prescribed medications. Unit-dose packaging systems provide individual doses for each administration time, ensuring accuracy and minimizing confusion. These are ideal for patients with complex regimens. Multi-dose packaging systems group multiple doses together, often by day or week, which simplifies medication management for patients with chronic conditions. Both types serve to improve adherence, reduce medication errors, and support better health outcomes through organized and easily accessible medication delivery.</p></p>
