<p><p>The brain tumor treatment market includes various modalities, primarily focusing on chemotherapy and targeted therapy. Chemotherapy involves the use of cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing tumor cells, often administered intravenously or orally. Targeted therapy, on the other hand, employs drugs designed to specifically target molecular abnormalities within tumor cells, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Both approaches can be used alone or in combination, tailored to the patient's specific tumor type and genetic profile to enhance treatment efficacy.</p></p>
