<p><p>The automotive windscreen lifters market comprises two primary types: electrically-operated windshield lifters and mechanical windshield lifters. Electrically-operated lifters utilize electric motors for smooth, controlled movement, enhancing convenience and user experience. They are often found in modern vehicles, providing features like one-touch operation. In contrast, mechanical windshield lifters rely on manual mechanisms, requiring physical effort to adjust the windshield. While typically more cost-effective, they may offer less precision and ease of use, catering to simpler or older vehicle models.</p></p>
