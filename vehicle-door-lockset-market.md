<p><strong>The Vehicle Door Lockset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pin Tumbler</li><li>Wafer Tumbler</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle door lockset market primarily consists of three types: pin tumbler, wafer tumbler, and others. Pin tumbler locks utilize a series of pins that must be aligned to unlock, providing higher security and resistance to picking. Wafer tumbler locks operate with flat wafers that slide aside when the correct key is inserted, offering simpler mechanisms often used in less security-critical applications. The "others" category encompasses advanced locking systems like electronic or smart locks, incorporating enhanced features and technologies for modern vehicles.</p></p>
