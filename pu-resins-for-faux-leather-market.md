<p><p>The PU resins for faux leather market encompasses two main types: dry-process synthetic leather and wet-process synthetic leather. Dry-process synthetic leather involves a solvent-free method where PU is applied to a substrate, offering a lightweight and environmentally friendly option with high durability. In contrast, wet-process synthetic leather utilizes a solvent-based approach, providing a more flexible and softer texture, often resembling genuine leather closely. Both processes cater to diverse applications, including fashion, automotive, and upholstery, enhancing design versatility and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
