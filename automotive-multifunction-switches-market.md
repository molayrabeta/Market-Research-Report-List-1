<p><p>The automotive multifunction switches market is divided into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket. OEM switches are designed and produced by vehicle manufacturers, ensuring compatibility and reliability as part of the original vehicle assembly. In contrast, aftermarket switches are produced by third-party companies and are often used for replacements or upgrades. They may offer more variety and cost-effective options, catering to consumers looking to enhance functionality or aesthetics beyond the original specifications.</p></p>
