<p><p>A Retro-Reflective Photoelectric Sensor is a type of sensor that utilizes a light beam, typically a laser or LED, which is emitted from the sensor and directed towards a reflective target. When the light beam hits the reflector, it is bounced back to the receiving element of the sensor, detecting objects even at a distance. This sensor type is widely used in industrial automation for presence detection, object counting, and safety applications.</p><p>The Retro-Reflective Photoelectric Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing automation in various industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and packaging.
