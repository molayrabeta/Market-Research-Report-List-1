<p><p>The single-phase capacitors market is primarily categorized into radial and axial types, each designed for specific applications. Radial capacitors feature leads extending from the ends, making them suitable for PCB mounting in compact spaces. They are commonly used in power supply circuits and motor applications. Axial capacitors, on the other hand, have leads on either end, allowing for efficient use of space in linear configurations. They are often utilized in audio equipment and specialized electronic devices, providing reliability and performance.</p></p>
