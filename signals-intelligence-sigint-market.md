<p><p>Signals Intelligence (SIGINT) involves the collection and analysis of electronic signals to gather intelligence. It encompasses two main market types: Electronic Intelligence (ELINT) and Communications Intelligence (COMINT). ELINT focuses on non-communication signals, such as radar emissions, to assess enemy capabilities and intentions. In contrast, COMINT deals with intercepted communications, including phone calls and emails, to understand adversary plans and operations. Both segments are crucial for national security and defense strategies, aiding in decision-making and operational planning.</p></p>
