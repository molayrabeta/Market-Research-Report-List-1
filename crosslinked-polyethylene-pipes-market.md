<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Crosslinked Polyethylene Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A Pipes</li><li>PEX-B Pipes</li><li>PEX-C Pipes</li><li>PEX-AL-PEX Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crosslinked polyethylene pipes market comprises four main types: PEX-A, PEX-B, PEX-C, and PEX-AL-PEX pipes. PEX-A pipes, known for superior flexibility and resistance to cracking, are produced using the Engel method. PEX-B pipes are created through the silane method, offering excellent chemical resistance and cost-effectiveness. PEX-C pipes utilize irradiation for crosslinking, providing good thermal stability and pressure resistance. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine polyethylene with an aluminum layer for enhanced strength and reduced oxygen permeability, making them ideal for heating systems.</p></p>
