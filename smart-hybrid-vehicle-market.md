<p><strong>The Smart Hybrid Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt-integrated Starter Generator</li><li>Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Hybrid Vehicle market features two main types of systems: Belt-integrated Starter Generators (BISGs) and Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generators (CMSGs). BISGs use a belt-driven mechanism to start the engine and assist with power generation, offering improved efficiency and reduced emissions. CMSGs are directly integrated into the crankshaft, providing seamless power delivery and energy recovery during deceleration. Both types enhance vehicle performance and fuel efficiency, contributing to the growing demand for eco-friendly transportation solutions.</p></p>
