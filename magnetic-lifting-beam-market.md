<p><p>The magnetic lifting beam market is segmented into two primary types: retractable and permanent. Permanent beams, on the other hand, utilize permanent magnets, providing consistent lifting capacity without the need for power sources, making them ideal for heavy-duty lifting tasks. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, optimizing material handling and improving safety in operations.</p></p>
