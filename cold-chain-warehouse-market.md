<p><strong>The Cold Chain Warehouse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chilled Warehousing</li><li>Frozen Warehousing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold chain warehouse market is divided into two primary types: chilled warehousing and frozen warehousing. Chilled warehousing maintains temperatures between 2°C to 8°C, ideal for perishable goods like dairy, fruits, and vegetables that require moderate cooling to preserve freshness. In contrast, frozen warehousing operates at temperatures below -18°C, suitable for long-term storage of frozen foods, meats, and pharmaceuticals. Both types ensure product quality and safety throughout the supply chain, playing essential roles in food distribution and temperature-sensitive logistics.</p></p>
