<p><p>The Composite Hydrogen Cylinder market is categorized into Type III and Type IV cylinders, primarily used for storage and transportation of hydrogen. Type III cylinders consist of an aluminum liner wrapped with fiberglass, offering a balance of weight and strength. They are suitable for applications requiring high pressure but moderate lightweight properties. Conversely, Type IV cylinders feature a plastic liner encased entirely in composite materials, providing superior lightweight advantages ideal for portable and high-capacity applications, enhancing efficiency in hydrogen distribution and utilization.</p></p>
