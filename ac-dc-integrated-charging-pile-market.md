<p><p>The AC-DC Integrated Charging Pile market encompasses two primary charging types: Constant Current Charging and Constant Voltage Charging. Constant Current Charging delivers a steady flow of current to the electric vehicle's battery, ensuring efficient charging without exceeding safe limits. In contrast, Constant Voltage Charging maintains a fixed voltage, adjusting the current as needed to prevent overcharging. Both methods aim to optimize battery lifespan and performance, catering to diverse user needs and adapting to various vehicle technologies in the growing electric vehicle market.</p></p>
