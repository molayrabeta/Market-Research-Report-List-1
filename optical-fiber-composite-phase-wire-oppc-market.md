<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) is a technology that incorporates optical fibers within overhead power lines, enhancing communication capabilities while maintaining electrical functions. The market can be segmented into two main types: Central Tube Structure OPPC, which features optical fibers housed centrally in a tubular design, and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC, in which fibers are distributed in layers around the conductor. Both types provide different mechanical and performance advantages, catering to diverse infrastructure needs in power and communication.</p></p>
